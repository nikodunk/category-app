More Sustainable Cosmetics Companies

The user-submitted directory of the best sustainable beauty companies

Animal testing is an ugly side of the beauty industry. These cruelty free makeup brands do not test their cosmetics on animals and many of them use vegan, natural ingredients that are good for you and good for the earth. We're aiming for a collection of cruelty free makeup brands to span a range of prices and types of products. The list is updated by you, so please share share your favorite cruelty free brand in the comments below, or add it to the list!
